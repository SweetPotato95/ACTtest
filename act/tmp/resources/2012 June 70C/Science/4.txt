<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Scientists investigated whether sleep improves procedural memory (memory of skilled movements).</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Subjects trained to tap their fingers in 1 or 2 sequences: Sequence X only, or Sequence X followed by Sequence Y (see Figure 1). To train for a sequence, the subjects attempted to tap the sequence for 30 sec and then rested for 30 sec, 12 times in succession. During the last 3 of these 12 cycles, the subjects' accuracy was tested.</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;"><img src='file:\$$$\resources\2012 June 70C\imgs\193.png' width = '522'height = '487'/></div>

<br>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Figure 1</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">The subjects' accuracy was retested—either immediately after training or after a full night's sleep—as they attempted to tap the sequence during 3 cycles.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Six groups, each consisting of 15 people, were subjected to different protocols:</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 1 trained for Seq X and was immediately retested</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 2 trained for Seq X, slept overnight, and was retested the next day.</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 3 trained for Seq X, immediately trained for Seq Y, and was immediately retested.</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 4 trained for Seq X, immediately trained for Seq Y, slept overnight, and was retested the next day.</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 5 trained for Seq X, waited for 6 hr, trained for Seq Y, slept overnight, and was retested the next day.</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Group 6 trained for Seq X, waited for 9 hr, trained for Seq Y, slept overnight, and was retested the next day.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">The table shows, for each group, the percent change in the average accuracy of the subjects between their initial testing (during training) and their retesting.</div>
