<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Four researchers performed the following experiment to investigate changes in snowshoe hare populations in a coniferous forest over an 8 yr period.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Experiment</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">The researchers selected a 10 km×10 km study area. Twenty-five 1 km×1 km plots were then mapped out within this study area, so that none of the plots was in direct contact with another plot. Each plot was then assigned to 1 of 5 treatments, as shown in Table 1.</div>
