<br>
<div>Write a unified, coherent essay in which you evaluate multiple perspectives on the increasing presence of intelligent machines. In your essay, be sure to:
　</div>
<ul>
<li>analyze and evaluate the perspectives given
</li>
<li>state and develop your own perspective on the issue</li>

</li>
