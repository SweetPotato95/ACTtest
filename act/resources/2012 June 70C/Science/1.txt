To determine whether these conditions are optimal for the growth of Bacteria X, the scientist conducted an experiment in which incubation temperature, and the pH and the NaCl concentration of the growth medium, were varied. In each trial, Bacteria X cells were incubated for 72 hr, and colony growth was then assayed by determining the average colony diameter (ACD) of the colonies that grew during the incubation (see the table).</div>
