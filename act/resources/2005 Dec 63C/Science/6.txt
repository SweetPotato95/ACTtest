<div class = "para">A student performed 4 computer simulation studies to model the change at a gene in a population. For each study, the following rules applied:</div>
<div class = "para">The gene has 2 alleles (possible forms): A and a.</div>
<div class = "para">Each organism has 2 copies of the gene.</div>
<div class = "para">Thus, there ate 3 possible genotypes (allele combinations for the gene): AA, Aa, and aa.</div>
<div class = "para">Different genotypes may have different fitnesses (the relative number of alleles per individual a genotype contributes to the next generation).</div>
<div class = "para">The fitnesses of Genotypes AA, Aa, and aa are represented by W(AA), W(Aa), and W(aa), respectively.</div>
<div class = "para">p = the proportion of the alleles that are in Form A.</div>
<div class = "para">Changes in p are due to differences in the fitnesses of the genotypes.</div>
<div class = "para">Time is measured in generations.</div>
