<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Challenge</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Our minds become sharper when faced with mental challenges; however, a line should be drawn to prevent unhealthy pressure. The challenges we choose to take on, whether a project, an opportunity, etc., can boost us to new levels of achievements or lead directly to failure. When should we seek challenges and when should we avoid them? It is worth considering our choices when we set a goal or come across a new opportunity in our lives.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Perspective 1:</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Life presents us enough challenges. Seeking challenges beyond this will bring us stress and unhappiness.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Perspective2:</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Challenges will lead us to realize our life potential goals. It is our opportunities to push our limits.</div>

<br>
<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Perspective3:</div>

<div class = "para" style = "font-size: 16;font-family:Microsoft YAHEI;">Wisdom is the result of discovering the difference between self-challenges that benefit us and those that do not.</div>
